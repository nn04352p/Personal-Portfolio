About Me

Hi! I'm Natalia Navarro, a finance student at Pace University's Lubin School of Business, currently pursuing my Bachelor of Business Administration with an expected graduation in May 2028. I'm passionate about financial management, investment strategies, and using my organizational skills to drive success.

My Journey

My professional journey has been shaped by hands-on experience in sales, management, and customer service. Currently, I work as a Sales Coordinator at Holiday Inn Express and Suites in Danbury, CT, where I lead the sales department to drive revenue growth and cultivate lasting client relationships. Previously, as a Jewelry Manager at Kohl's Department Store, I honed my leadership abilities while managing inventory, optimizing displays, and enhancing the customer experience.

Academic Excellence

At Pace University, I've maintained a 3.75 GPA and earned a place on the Dean's List for Fall 2024 and Spring 2025. My coursework in economics, financial accounting, business law, and contemporary business practices has given me a strong foundation in the principles that guide successful organizations.

Community & Leadership

Beyond academics and work, I'm deeply committed to giving back to my community. As a leader for Habitat for Humanity, I guided fundraising initiatives and club projects that made meaningful improvements for underserved communities. I've also volunteered at kindergarten book fairs, encouraging early literacy and helping young students build confidence in their reading abilities.

Looking Forward

I'm always seeking new opportunities to learn, grow, and make an impact. Whether it's through finance, leadership, or mentorship, I believe in the power of hard work, organization, and genuine connection with others. I'm excited to continue building my career and contributing to organizations that value innovation and excellence.
